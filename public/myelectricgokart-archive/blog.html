New Circuit Breaker (again!)

12/1/2010

0 Comments

 
Well, I was in California with my family, visiting some friends and having Thanksgiving there, and I went to this awesome little electronics store, Electronics Plus. They have everything there! It's really cool. If you're into that kind of stuff, I highly recommend checking that store out next time you might be in the San Francisco bay area. Anyway, from them, I bought another new circuit breaker, because my other one kept popping. My new one is 4o amps instead of 3o amps. So *hopefully* it will work better then the last one. It was also only about $8+tax to buy, so its not like it was a huge investment. I have yet to install it however, but I'll keep you posted.
